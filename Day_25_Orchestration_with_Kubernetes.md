# Day 25: Orchestration with Kubernetes

## Learning Objectives
- Core k8s concepts: Pods, Deployments, Services, ConfigMaps, Secrets
- Scaling, rolling updates, and health probes
- Networking basics (Services, Ingress) and RBAC

## Why This Matters
Kubernetes is the de facto platform for deploying and operating containerized workloads at scale.

---

## Hands-On Exercise
- Deploy a simple app to a local cluster (Kind / Minikube) with Deployment, Service, and Ingress.

## Mini Project
- Implement a canary or blue-green deployment using k8s primitives and measure rollbacks.

## Expected Outcome
You can deploy, scale, and manage containerized apps in Kubernetes.
