# Day 14: CAP Theorem, Replication, and Consistency Models

## Learning Objectives
- Learn CAP theorem and its practical implications
- Understand replication strategies: master-slave, multi-master
- Consistency models: strong, eventual, causal

## Prerequisites
- Days 10-13

## Why This Matters
Distributed databases make trade-offs between availability, consistency, and partition tolerance—critical for global systems.

---

## Hands-On Exercise

- Set up PostgreSQL streaming replication (master -> replica)
- Simulate network partition and observe behaviors

---

## Mini Project
Design a replicated datastore for global reads with eventual consistency and explain conflict resolution strategies.

---

## Expected Outcome
You can choose replication and consistency models based on system requirements.
