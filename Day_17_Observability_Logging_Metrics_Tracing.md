# Day 17: Observability — Logging, Metrics, and Tracing

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the three pillars: logs, metrics, traces
- Learn common tools: Prometheus, Grafana, ELK/EFK, Jaeger
- Instrument services for observability

## Why This Matters
Observability is essential for diagnosing production issues and understanding system behavior.

---

## Hands-On Exercise
- Instrument a simple web service with Prometheus metrics and visualise in Grafana.
- Add structured logging (JSON) and send logs to a local ELK/EFK stack or Loki.

## Mini Project
- Trace a request through multiple services with OpenTelemetry and Jaeger.

## Expected Outcome
You can instrument apps for logs/metrics/traces and build basic dashboards and traces.
