# Day 23: Secrets Management and Identity & Access Management (IAM)

## Learning Objectives
- Learn secure storage and rotation of secrets (HashiCorp Vault, AWS Secrets Manager)
- Principle of least privilege and IAM policies
- Short-lived credentials and workload identity

## Why This Matters
Improper secret handling leads to breaches and lateral movement risks.

---

## Hands-On Exercise
- Store a database password in Vault or a local secrets manager and fetch it dynamically in an app.

## Mini Project
- Implement secret rotation and audit logging for access to sensitive credentials.

## Expected Outcome
You can manage secrets securely and design IAM policies following least privilege.
